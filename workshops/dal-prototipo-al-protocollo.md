# From Prototype to Protocol

**Layer**: 05 · STRATEGY — Strategic Protocol  
**Duration**: 3 hours

---

## Objective

Distill from the validated prototypes the decision principles that will constitute the brand's strategic protocol.

## How It Works

The Synthesis Circle works on three levels:

1. From the constellation of prototypes and values, it builds the brand's **narrative thesis**.
2. It translates the thesis into **operational choice criteria**: the rules of thumb for evaluating future decisions.
3. It defines an **experiment roadmap** at 30/60/90 days to test hypotheses in real contexts before they become irreversible choices.

## Expected Output

Strategic Protocol with narrative thesis, decision criteria, and experiment roadmap.
